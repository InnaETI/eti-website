## 2. What we do (in plain language)

ETI is a **consulting and advisory firm** that helps organizations — especially in **healthcare** and **complex regulated environments** — when they need to:

- **Decide** what technology and operating model changes matter (strategy, budget, platforms).
- **Design** how teams and governance should work so plans are executable (methodology, agile, change).
- **Deliver** so business intent survives implementation pressure (execution, hands-on guidance).

The site repeatedly frames three **pillars**: **Strategy**, **Methodology**, and **Execution** — not as three separate vendors, but as one continuous way of working with clients.

**Where ETI says it fits best (from homepage):**

- When **leadership intent**, **delivery pressure**, **budget reality**, and **technical complexity** pull in different directions.
- When you need **executive alignment before delivery chaos compounds**.
- When you want **technology strategy tied to operational priorities**, not slide decks.
- When you want **hands-on guidance from planning through execution**.

**Operating principle (homepage quote):**  
*Transformation work fails when strategy, team realities, and execution sequencing are treated as separate problems.*

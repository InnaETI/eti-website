**Client summaries (outcome-style for Proof section):**

- **Healthcare Payment Specialists (HPS):** Technology-enabled eligibility, government reimbursement, compliance; SaaS and outsourced engagements. ETI assessment and improvements to Stingray Platform.  
- **ExplORer Surgical:** Interactive surgical playbook; teamwork in OR; learning health system.  
- **Trainerly:** Connects clients to fitness professionals via webcam; group workout classes.  
- **StratusVue:** Collaborative software for commercial and institutional construction.
